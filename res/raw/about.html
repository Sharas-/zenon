ZenOn will help you to return to the present moment

Start the app and you will get alerts at random times. When you get an alert, return from your thoughts into being here and now. Remain present as long as you can.

Adjust your mindfulness level based on how easy it is to remain present for you. The lower the mindfulness level you set, the more frequently and periodically you will get an alert.
